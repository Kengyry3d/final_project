The football club Tottenham Hotspur is an English professional football club from Tottenham, a district in the north of London, playing in the Premier League. Founded in 1882. The nickname of the club - “spurs”. Home stadium of the club - "Wembley".

Tottenham Hotspur became the first club in the XX century, making a home “double”, winning both the championship and the FA Cup in the 1960/1961 season. In 1963, the Spurs became the first British club to win the European Cup - the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup. In the 1980s, the Spurs won several trophies: twice the FA Cup, the English Super Cup, and the UEFA Cup (1983/1984). In 2008, the club won the Football League Cup, beating in the Chelsea finals 2-1 in extra time; Thus, Tottenham won the title at least once a decade for the past 60 years, as did Manchester United.

The 2008 Football League Cup Final is a football match that took place on February 24, 2008. It was the first League Cup Final to be held at the new Wembley Stadium after the demolition of the old Wembley 2000. The match was the end of the 48th Football League Cup, the cup tournament, in which 92 clubs from the Premier League and Football League take part. The final was broadcast live on BBC and Sky Sports. In the final, met two Tottenham Hotspur and Chelsea clubs in London. Tottenham Hotspur defeated Chelsea 2-1 in extra time, winning his first trophy in 9 years.
