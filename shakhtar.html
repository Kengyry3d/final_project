Our Work

Shakhtar is a Ukrainian football club from the city of Donetsk, playing in the Ukrainian Premier League. The first Ukrainian club to win the UEFA Cup. Eleven-time champion of Ukraine, twelve-time winner of the Cup of Ukraine, eight-time winner of the Super Cup of Ukraine, four-time winner of the USSR Cup.

Until 2014, the team held their home games at the Donbass Arena stadium, which was opened on August 29, 2009. Because of the armed conflict in the east of Ukraine, the matches in Donetsk were discontinued, since 2014 the team has played their home games in Lviv at the Arena Lviv stadium. Since 2017, the team holds home matches in Kharkov at the Metalist stadium.

Win 1

Tournament

The 2009 UEFA Cup Final is the final match of the 2008/09 UEFA Cup draw, the 38th season in UEFA Cup history. This football match took place on May 20, 2009, at the “Sukru Saracoglu” stadium in Istanbul. In the match, the Ukrainian “Shakhtar” (Donetsk) and the German “Werder” (Bremen) met. Where Shakhtar won the Werder Bremen with a score 2:1.

Win 2

Tournament

Championship of Ukraine on football among teams of clubs of the Premier League season 2017/2018 - 27th championship of Ukraine on football. The season began on July 15, 2017 with the match for the Supercup of Ukraine between Shakhtar Donetsk and Dynamo Kyiv. The championship started on July 16, 2017, the match between Karpaty and Zvezda (1: 1). The champion title was defended by Shakhtar Donetsk.

Win 3

Tournament

Ukrainian Cup 2017/2018 - the 27th draw of the Cup of Ukraine, which was held from July 9, 2017 to May 9, 2018. In final Shakhtar won the Dinamo Kiev with a score 2:0.
