Paris Saint-Germain, PSG - a professional French football club from Paris. Founded in 1970, playing in Ligue 1. “Paris Saint-Germain” became the champion of France seven times, won the French Cup twelve times and the French League Cup eight times. The club achieved success in the European arena by winning the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup, beating the Rapid in the final in Vienna.
