UEFA Champions League

The UEFA Champions League is an annual club football competition organised by the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) and contested by top-division European clubs. It is one of the most prestigious tournaments in the world and the most prestigious club competition in European football, played by the national league champions (and, for some nations, one or more runners-up) of the strongest UEFA national associations.

Format

  • Qualifying rounds: 27 June - 2 August 2017.
    • 64 teams
    • Matches to one victory
    • Round 5 matches are Bo3
    • The winner passes to the next round
    • 32 teams finish the fight
  • Group stage: 12 September - 6 December 2017.
    • 32 teams
    • 8 groups of 4 teams
    • The first two teams in the group go on the rest stop fighting
  • Play-off round: 13 February 2017 - 26 May 2018.
    • Match Bo2 except the final
    • After two matches, the team leaves the tournament.
