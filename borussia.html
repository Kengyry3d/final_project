Borussia Dortmund Football Club is a professional German football club from Dortmund. Being a multiple champion of Germany, winner of the Cup Winners' Cup and winner of the Champions League, Borussia is one of the most titled football clubs in Germany. A member of the G-14 league until its dissolution in 2008.

Win 1

Tournament

The 1997 Intercontinental Cup is a football match held on December 2, 1997 between Borussia, the winner of the UEFA Champions League, and Cruzeiro, the winner of the Libertadores Cup. The match was played in a neutral field at the National Stadium in Tokyo. It was visited by 46 953 spectators. Player mast was recognized by Andreas Moeller. In final Borussia Dortmund won the Cruzeiro with a score 2:0.

The 1997 UEFA Champions League Final is the final match of the UEFA Champions League 1996/97, the 42nd season in the history of the European Champions Cup and the 5th season in the history of the UEFA Champions League. This football match took place on May 28, 1997, at the Olympiastadion stadium in Munich. In the match met the German "Borussia Dortmund" and the Italian "Juventus". In final Borussia Dortmund won the Juventus with a score 3:1;
