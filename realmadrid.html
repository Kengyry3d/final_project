Real Madrid is a Spanish professional football club from the city of Madrid. FIFA recognized as the best football club of the 20th century. “Real Madrid” is one of three clubs that have never left the highest Spanish division, the other two are Barcelona and Athletic Bilbao. Is one of the most titled clubs in Spanish football. He has 62 national titles: a record 33 titles of La Liga champion, 19 Spanish Cups and 10 Spanish Super Cups. He holds the record for the number of victories and goals in the Champions League (13 times, the only team that won this tournament - then the European Champions Cup - 5 times in a row). According to the organization Deloitte, in the season 2016/17 Real Madrid was the second in terms of earnings among clubs with an annual income of 674.6 million euros. It is one of the most valuable clubs valued at 4.1 billion dollars.

Tournament

The final of the Club World Cup 2018 is the final match of the Club World Cup 2018, which was held in the United Arab Emirates. This final was the fifteenth in the history of the World Cup World Cup. The final was drawn between the Spanish club Real Madrid, representing UEFA as the winner of the Champions League 2017/18, and the Emirates club "Al Ain" from Abu Dhabi, representing the host country as the winner League of the Arabian Gulf 2017/18. The match took place on December 22, 2018 at the Zayed Sport City Stadium in the city of Abu Dhabi. The winner was Real Madrid, which beat Al Ain with a score 4:1.

Win 2

Tournament

The UEFA Champions League Final 2018 is the 2017/18 UEFA Champions League final match, the 63rd season of the most prestigious UEFA club football tournament, and the 26th season after the European Champions Cup was renamed the Champions League. The match took place on May 26, 2018 in Kiev, for the first time in the history of Ukraine, at the stadium Olympic NSC. The winner was Real Madrid, which beat English Liverpool with a score 3:1.

Win 3

Tournament

UEFA Super Cup 2017 - a football match that took place on August 8, 2017 and became the 42nd draw of the UEFA Super Cup, in which the winner of the Champions League and the European League of the previous season met. The match was held at the stadium "National Arena Philip II of Macedon" in Skopje. The winner was Real Madrid, which beat English Manchester United with a score of 2:1.
