Schalke 04 is a German football club from Gelsenkirchen, North Rhine-Westphalia. One of the most popular football clubs in Germany. In September 2018, the number of club members crossed the mark of 156,000 people, and according to this indicator, Schalke 04 ranks second in Germany. The club achieved the best result in European competitions in 1997, winning the UEFA Cup.

Win 2

Tournament

German Soccer Cup 2010–2011 - 68th German Soccer Cup. The tournament began on August 13, 2010, the final was played on May 21, 2011 at the Olympic Stadium in Berlin. The title was defended by a 15-time winner of the cup - Bavaria. In the final match, Schalke 04 won the second Bundesliga club Duisburg with a score of 5:0 and won the German Cup for the fifth time.

Win 3

Tournament

German Super Cup 2011 - the 12th official Super Bowl of Germany, which has been held annually since 2010. In the German Super Cup there are champions of the Bundesliga and winners of the German Cup of the previous season. The match took place on July 23, 2011 at the Veltins Arena stadium in Gelsenkirchen. In it there were irreconcilable rivals, the champion of the Bundesliga Dortmund “Borussia” and the winner of the German Cup Gelsenkirchen “Schalke 04 ″. The main time ended with a score of 0:0, and in the penalty shootout, Schalke 04 won with a score of 4:3.
