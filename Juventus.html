Juventus is an Italian professional football club from Turin, one of the oldest, most titled and strongest clubs in Italy, Europe and the world. In 1988, the club received the UEFA award for victories in all tournaments held by the federation. When Juventus won the UEFA Super Cup in 1984, the Intercontinental Cup in 1985 and the Intertoto Cup in 1999, it became the only club in the world to submit to all tournaments.

In September 2012, a survey was conducted, which showed that Juventus is the most popular team in Italy. In addition, according to the company Sport + Markt AG, 130 million people are ill with Juventus in the world.
