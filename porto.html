Football club "Porto" - Portuguese football club from the city of the same name. The club’s home stadium is “Drahau”, built in 2003 in preparation for Euro 2004.

Porto, together with Benfica and Sporting Lisbon, form the Big Three in Portugal. Porto twice won the Champions League and once in the UEFA Cup and Europa League. The club was the first to repeat the Liverpool result in 1976-1977, winning the UEFA Cup first and then becoming the first in the Champions League.

The 2004 UEFA Champions League Final is the final match of the UEFA Champions League 2003/04, the 49th season in the history of the European Champions Cup and the 12th season in the history of the UEFA Champions League. This football match took place on May 26, 2004, at the Arena Aufschalk stadium in Gelsenkirchen. The match met the French "Monaco" and the Portuguese "Porto". In final Porty won the Monaco with a score 3:0.

The 2011 UEFA Europa League Final is the final match of the UEFA Europa League 2010/11, the 40th season in the history of the UEFA Cup and the 2nd season in the history of the UEFA Europa League. This football match took place on May 18, 2011, at the Dublin Arena stadium in Dublin. The match met the Portuguese "Porto" and "Braga". In final Porty won the Braga with a score 1:0.
