Our Work

Roma is an Italian sports association from Rome. The colors of the club are golden yellow and purple red. Home matches are held at the "Stadio Olimpico", accommodating 72,698 spectators. Roma has participated in all seasons of Serie A since its inception (1929/30) except for one season in Serie B (1951/52). Roma is a member of the Association of European Clubs.

Win 1

Tournament

Serie A 2000/2001 - 69th season of the Italian Football Championship. The first matches took place on September 30, 2000, the last - on June 17, 2001. “Roma” 3 times in its history won the title of champion, it was her first championship since the season 1982/83.

Win 2

Tournament

Italian Football Super Cup 2007 - the 20th draw of the Italian Super Cup, in which the champion of the season 2006/07 Milan Inter and the Italian Cup 2006/07 Roman Roma met. The meeting took place on August 19, 2007 in Milan, at the stadium Giuseppe Meazza. In final Poma won the Inter with a score 1:0.

Win 3

Tournament

UEFA Cup 1990/91 - 20th draw of the UEFA Cup, the winner was Inter, who beat Roma in the final. It was the second Italian UEFA Cup final. This rally was notable for the return of English clubs to European cups after a five-year boycott, caused by the Eiselian tragedy.
