Athletico Madrid is a Spanish football club from Madrid, in the autonomous community of the same name. The club was founded on April 26, 1903, it receives guests at the Vanda Metropolitano stadium. Atletico is the ten-time champion of Spain, the ten-time winner of the Spanish Cup, as well as the three-time winner of the UEFA Europa League seasons 2009/10, 2011/12 and 2017/18.

Win 1

Tournament

UEFA Europa League 2017/18 is the 47th draw of the annual club tournament in European football and the 9th called the Europa League. The final match took place on May 16, 2018 at the Park Olympic Lyonnais Stadium in Lyon. The winner was the club "Atletico Madrid" - this is his third trophy of this European Cup.

Win 2

Tournament

UEFA Super Cup 2018 - a football match that took place on August 15, 2018 and became the 43rd draw of the UEFA Super Cup, in which the winner of the Champions League and the European League of the previous season met. The match was held at the stadium “A. Le Coq Arena "in Tallinn. The winner was Atletico Madrid, which beat Real Madrid 4-2.
