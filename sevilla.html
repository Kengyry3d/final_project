Sevilla is a Spanish professional football club from the city of the same name. It was formed on January 25, 1890. Currently conference in Example. Home games are held at the Ramon Sanchez Pizhuang Stadium, which seats 45,500 spectators. The colors of the club are red and white.

Win 1

Tournament

Spanish Soccer Cup 2009-2010 - the 106th Spanish Football Cup draw held by the Royal Spanish Football Federation. The winner of the Spanish Cup 5 times became Seville.

Win 2

Tournament

UEFA Europa League 2015/16 - the 45th draw of the annual club tournament in European football and the 7th under the name UEFA Europa League. The final match took place on May 18, 2016 at the stadium St. Jakob Park in Basel. The winners of the tournament in the 3rd consecutive time became the Spanish Seville.

Win 3

Tournament

UEFA Super Cup 2006 - the 31st draw of the UEFA Super Cup. The winner in the game was the UEFA Champions League 2005–2006 Spanish “Barcelona” and the winner of the UEFA Cup 2005–2006 Spanish “Seville”. The match took place at the Louis II Stadium in Monaco on August 25, 2006. At the end of the match, the UEFA Super Cup won Sevilla for the first time in history. In final Sevilla won the Barcelona withe a score 3:0.
