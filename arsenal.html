Arsenal Football Club is an English professional football club from North London, playing in the Premier League. Founded in 1886. The club became the champion of England 13 times, won the England Cup 13 times. The club's home stadium is Emirates, which seats 59,867 spectators. Arsenal spent most of its history in the top division of English football, and in 1992 became one of the founding clubs of the English Premier League. The club also holds the record for the number of seasons in a row spent in the top division of the championship of England, starting in 1919.
