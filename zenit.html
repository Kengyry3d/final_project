Football Club Zenit , also known as Zenit Saint Petersburg or simply Zenit, is a Russian football club from the city of Saint Petersburg. Founded in 1925, the club plays in the Russian Premier League. Zenit were the 2007, 2010, 2011–12 and 2014–15 champions of the Russian Premier League and the winners of both the 2007–08 UEFA Cup and the 2008 UEFA Super Cup. The club is owned and sponsored by a Russian state-owned energy giant Gazprom.

UEFA Cup 2007/08 - The 37th Cup. The final match took place on May 14, 2008 at the City of Manchester stadium in Manchester between the Zenit and Rangers teams and ended with a Zenit victory with a score of 2:0.

The XXIII Russian Football Championship among Premier League clubs was held in 2014–2015. The third season, which was organized according to the “autumn-spring” system. The champion title was defended by Moscow CSKA, Zenit became the champion for 2 rounds to the end. The championship began on August 1, 2014 with the Rubin-Spartak match, and ended on May 30, 2015.
