Football club "Bayern" Munich - a professional German football club from the city of Munich. Founded in 1900. The most titled club in Germany and one of the most titled clubs in the world.

Bayern took 3rd place in the list of the best football clubs of the 20th century, according to FIFA, 4th place - according to Kicker magazine. IFFHS also placed Bayern in 5th place in the ranking of the best European clubs of the twentieth century.

Bayern is the most award-winning professional football club in Germany, it has 28 champion titles and 19 German cups, 3 wins in the European Cup and 2 wins in the Champions League. At the end of 2013, the team became the first German club to win the club world championship.
